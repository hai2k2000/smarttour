# Product Context

SmartTour users are tour operators, operation managers, accountants, and business owners. The app should help them see what needs action, which suppliers are assigned, which costs are pending, and whether a tour is profitable.

## Users

- Operation manager: controls tour execution and supplier coordination.
- Operator: updates operation services, tasks, and confirmations.
- Accountant: reviews costs and supplier payment requests.
- Manager/CEO: reviews operational risk and profit/loss.

## Product Bias

Prioritize useful operational screens over broad module count. Every feature should reduce a spreadsheet, repeated message, missed confirmation, or unclear supplier debt.
